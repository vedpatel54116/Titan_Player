TitanPlayer is an open-source, native macOS media player focused on
high-fidelity playback of 4K HDR content, multi-window library browsing,
and AirPlay 2 multi-room output.

• Hardware-accelerated decode of H.264, HEVC, and AV1 (on supported Macs).
• Real-time HDR tone-mapping pipeline with per-display calibration.
• Multi-window library browsing — drag folders into their own window.
• AirPlay 2 sender with per-device buffering.
• Spatial Audio passthrough and external audio device routing.
• Picture-in-Picture and a floating Mini Player.
• Sidecar subtitle / closed-caption support with WebVTT cues.
• Playback position memory, resume from last frame.
• Open MIT-licensed code; no telemetry, no analytics, no upload of your media.

Runs on macOS 14 Sonoma or later, on Apple Silicon and Intel iGPUs.
